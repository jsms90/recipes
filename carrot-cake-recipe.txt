Carrot cake:

For the cake:
	175ml sunflower iol
	200 muscovado sugar
	3 large eggs
	140g carrots, grated
	175 self-raising flour
	1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
	1.5 tsp ground ginger
	0.5 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
	grated zest of 1 orange
	75g chopped walnuts (a few halved for decoration)

For the icing:
	200g full0fat cream cheese
	50g butter, softened
	85g icing sugar, sifted
	pinch of ground cinammon
